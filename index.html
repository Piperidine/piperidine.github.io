About

Freshman year has common courses for all departments, except a Department Introductory Course. In the future years, there are more of department specific courses and electives, and a student might need advice/assistance in the courses, projects or other department related information like interesting courses (electives etc.). With this in mind, DAMP attempts to make the academic life of all students in the institute easier, and makes sure, those who require support never lack in it.

We make sure that every student has a motivated and well rounded senior student from his/her own department, someone whom he/she can connect with, to be approached in case of academic needs/problems. We also help in bridging the gap between the student and the department and faculty (especially his/her faculty advisor). Also, we intend to obtain feedback from students regarding the current curriculum, so that our faculty gets to know the students perspective about academics.
